MeshLens Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 13, 2026

Developer: NextLab

MeshLens is a 3D model viewing and analysis application designed to allow users to open, inspect, and analyze 3D models directly on their devices.

1. Information We Collect

MeshLens does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information from users.

The application does not require users to create an account and does not collect names, email addresses, location data, contacts, or other personally identifiable information.

2. User Files and 3D Models

MeshLens processes 3D model files selected by the user on their local device. These files remain on the user's device and are not uploaded to external servers.

The developer does not access, store, or receive copies of user-created or user-opened 3D models.

3. Data Storage

MeshLens may store application settings or preferences locally on the user's device to provide a better user experience.

No user data is transmitted to the developer or any third-party service.

4. Analytics and Tracking

MeshLens does not use advertising networks, analytics services, tracking technologies, or third-party data collection tools.

5. Third-Party Services

MeshLens does not integrate with third-party services that collect user information.

6. Children's Privacy

MeshLens does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

7. Security

Since MeshLens does not collect or transmit personal information, there is no personal data stored on external servers by the application.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time to reflect improvements to the application or changes in requirements. Any updates will be published on this page.
